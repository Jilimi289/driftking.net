About Drift King

Drift King is an exhilarating online racing game that puts your drifting skills to the ultimate test. Experience the thrill of controlling powerful cars as you drift through challenging tracks and compete against opponents from around the world.

With stunning graphics, realistic physics, and a variety of cars to choose from, Drift King offers an immersive racing experience for both casual players and hardcore enthusiasts. Master each track, unlock new vehicles, and climb the global leaderboards to prove you're the true Drift King.

Whether you're looking for quick racing action or want to develop your skills over time, Drift King provides endless entertainment with its addictive gameplay and competitive challenges.

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • Accelerate Up Arrow / W
  • Brake/Reverse Down Arrow / S
  • Steer Left Left Arrow / A
  • Steer Right Right Arrow / D
  • Handbrake (Drift) Space
  • Nitro Boost Shift

Pro Tips

  • Perfect your timing when initiating drifts for maximum points
  • Use nitro strategically on straightaways to build momentum
  • Master countersteering to maintain control during long drifts
  • Chain drifts together for combo multipliers
